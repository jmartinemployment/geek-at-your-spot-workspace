## SEO Keyword Strategy

### Keyword Research Per Service

Before writing each blog post, identify:

**Primary Keywords (1-2):**
- Main service term: "cloud migration", "business analytics", "API integration"
- Include location: "cloud migration Florida", "business analytics Broward County"

**Secondary Keywords (3-5):**
- Service variations: "cloud infrastructure", "migrate to AWS", "cloud hosting"
- Benefits: "reduce server costs", "scalable cloud solutions"
- Technology specific: "AWS migration", "Azure cloud", "Google Cloud Platform"

**Long-tail Keywords (5-10):**
- Question-based: "how much does cloud migration cost", "what is cloud infrastructure"
- Problem-based: "reduce IT infrastructure costs", "eliminate server maintenance"
- Comparison: "cloud vs on-premise", "AWS vs Azure for small business"

### Keyword Placement Strategy

**Title (H1):** Include primary keyword
- ‚úÖ "Cloud Migration & Infrastructure: Stop Paying for Servers You Don't Need"
- ‚ùå "Moving to the Cloud: A Guide"

**First 100 Words:** Include primary keyword 1-2 times naturally

**Headers (H2/H3):** Include keyword variations
- "How Modern Cloud Infrastructure Works" ‚úÖ
- "Cloud vs. On-Premise Cost Comparison" ‚úÖ
- "When You Need Cloud Migration in Florida" ‚úÖ

**Throughout Content:**
- Primary keyword: 8-12 times (for ~2000 word post)
- Secondary keywords: 3-5 times each
- Long-tail keywords: Naturally in context
- Avoid keyword stuffing - maintain natural flow

**Location Keywords:**
- "Serving Broward & Palm Beach County, FL" (in footer)
- "Florida businesses", "South Florida companies"
- Mention in 1-2 sections naturally

### Semantic SEO

Include related terms Google associates with your topic:
- Cloud migration: "data center", "virtualization", "scalability", "uptime"
- Business analytics: "dashboard", "KPIs", "data visualization", "reporting"
- API integration: "endpoints", "webhooks", "REST API", "automation"

### Answer Search Intent

Structure content to answer common questions:
- **Informational**: "What is [service]?" ‚Üí Early sections explain concept
- **Problem-solving**: "How to [solve problem]?" ‚Üí Real-world examples
- **Comparison**: "[Option A] vs [Option B]" ‚Üí Dedicated comparison section
- **Local**: "[Service] near me" ‚Üí Location mentions, service area
- **Cost**: "How much does [service] cost?" ‚Üí Pricing overview (not detailed)

## Blog Post Tone & Style Guidelines

### Writing Style:
- **Direct and conversational** - Write like you're explaining to a business owner over coffee
- **Problem-focused opening** - Start with pain points they recognize
- **Demystify enterprise tech** - Explain that expensive solutions use accessible technology
- **Concrete examples** - Use real numbers, specific scenarios, actual ROI
- **Avoid jargon** - Explain technical terms in plain English
- **Short paragraphs** - 2-4 sentences max for readability
- **Strategic formatting** - Use bold for emphasis, bullets for clarity (not excessively)

### Content Structure:
1. **Hook with pain** - Start with problems they're experiencing
2. **Show enterprise reality** - How big companies solve it
3. **Bridge the gap** - Reveal it's accessible at their scale
4. **Technical credibility** - Show you know the tech stack
5. **Prove with examples** - Real results with real numbers
6. **Address concerns** - Security, integration, pricing
7. **Clear pricing** - Transparent tiers and ongoing costs
8. **Strong CTA** - Free assessment or consultation offer

### Key Phrases to Include:
- "You don't need their budget"
- "Same technology as [major company]"
- "Strip away the enterprise bloat"
- "Pays for itself in [timeframe]"
- Location mentions: "Florida businesses", "Broward County", "Palm Beach County" (in content, not footer)
- Footer: "Code Like a Legend" only
